Getting Around
Essential transport info for the trip

🚃 IC Card (Suica)

Tap-and-go card for all trains, metro and buses across Japan. Add to Apple Wallet before you fly or collect at Narita.

💡 Load ¥5,000–10,000 each on arrival. Top up at any ticket machine or 7-Eleven.

🚌 Hakone Freepass

Covers all transport within Hakone — ropeway, lake cruise, bus, local rail. Already booked via EMot app (Sarah). Romancecar supplement needed separately.

💡 Freepass × 4 already paid. Romancecar seats open Mon 9 Mar — book via EMot app.

🚄 Shinkansen

Odawara → Kyoto ~75 mins. Green Car seats open Tue 10 Mar on Klook (~¥13,000pp). Sit right side (D/E seats) for Mt Fuji views.

💡 Jack books his own ticket same service via Klook.

🚕 Taxis in Japan

Metered, honest, no tipping. Doors open automatically — don't touch them. Show destination in Japanese on your phone.

💡 Use GO app to book ahead and pay by card.

✈️ Airport Transfers

Narita → Shinjuku: Klook bus × 4 already paid (£91.28). Seoul taxis both ways confirmed via Tourmaker (Ref #1479-1 and #1479-2) — driver holds EVANS FAMILY banner.

💡 WhatsApp driver details arrive the evening before each transfer.
